CAT

The cat, commonly referred to as the domestic cat or house cat, is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae.

DOG

The dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf. Also called the domestic dog, it is derived from extinct gray wolves

MOUSE

A mouse is a small rodent. Characteristically, mice are known to have a pointed snout, small rounded ears, a body-length scaly tail.

COW

Cattle are large, domesticated, bovid ungulates widely kept as livestock. They are prominent modern members of the subfamily.
